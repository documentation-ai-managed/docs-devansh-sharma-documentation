## Account and Billing

Manage your Devansh Sharma Documentation account efficiently. You handle subscriptions, billing details, and access controls directly from the dashboard.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How do I upgrade my plan?">
    Navigate to your account settings. Select the desired plan from the billing section and confirm the upgrade. Changes take effect immediately.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    You can use credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. Update methods in the billing panel anytime.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can I cancel my subscription?">
    Yes, cancel from the billing page. You retain access until the end of the current cycle. No refunds for partial months.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Review your usage stats before upgrading to ensure the new plan fits your needs.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

Follow these steps to resolve most problems quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Status" icon="activity">
    Visit the dashboard status page. Look for outages or maintenance windows.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Clear Cache" icon="refresh-cw">
    Run the cache clear command:

````bash
npm run clear-cache
````

  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Logs" icon="file-text">
    Access build logs via the projects panel. Search for error keywords like `syntax` or `timeout`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact Support" icon="mail">
    Submit a ticket with logs attached if issues persist.
  </Step>
</Steps>
